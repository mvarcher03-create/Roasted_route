About Roasted Route

Founded in 2025, Roasted Route has quickly become Biliran's favorite destination for delicious, freshly roasted chicken. Our passion for quality and flavor drives everything we do.

We source our chickens from local farms, ensuring freshness and supporting our community. Our secret blend of herbs and spices has been perfected over years of experimentation.

At Roasted Route, we believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're dining in, taking out, or having it delivered, we promise a memorable culinary experience.
